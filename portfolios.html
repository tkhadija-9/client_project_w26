Portfolios

Craft a Portfolio that Tells Your Story

Your portfolio is way to showcase to highlight your unique strengths, skills, and experiences to potential employers. Whether you’re starting from scratch or looking to polish an existing portfolio, these resources are here to help you every step of the way.

UMSI students are in high demand, and a clear, well-presented portfolio will help you stand out among applicants. On this page, you'll find practical tips, templates, and guides—all tailored to the needs of School of Information students and aligned with employer expectations.

Need feedback? You can always meet with a CDO Career Coach to review your materials.
